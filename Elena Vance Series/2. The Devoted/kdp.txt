--- Description (back-cover blurb; use on all formats) ---
A celebrated philanthropist dies of her own sleeping pills and is ruled a
suicide. But when executive coach Elena Vance — a woman who reads people for a
living — meets the dead woman's inner circle, she notices that no one's face
matches the official story.

Everyone heard Maren Ash's signature music playing at midnight. Everyone
assumed she was awake. Everyone agrees she was sinking into despair.

Everyone is wrong.

To find the truth, Elena must prove a murder no one believes happened — hidden
inside a roomful of women who all loved the dead woman. Her only instrument is
her gift for reading people. And her one clue is the grief of a protégée whose
sorrow is a little too perfect.

Real grief is never correct.

The Devoted is the second Elena Vance novel — a fair-play psychological
detective story where the perfect murder is one no one knows is a crime.
