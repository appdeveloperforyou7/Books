# Book Layout & Design Guide (For Amazon KDP Publication)

As you noted, the design of this book is just as important as the text itself. To achieve a premium, magazine-quality feel that Amazon shoppers are willing to pay $5–$10 for, the integration of text and photography must be flawless.

---

## Recommended Book Specifications (Amazon KDP)

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| **Trim Size** | **7" × 10"** (ideal for photo-heavy premium books — more visual real estate) |
| **Interior Type** | Premium Color Print |
| **Image Resolution** | **300 DPI minimum** for all images. When downloading or generating, request at least **2,100 × 3,000 pixels**. |
| **Bleed Setting** | **0.125"** on all four sides (Enable bleed in Canva when exporting) |
| **Margins** | Minimum **0.5" inside margin**, 0.5" outside, 0.5" top/bottom |
| **Assembly Tool** | **Canva Pro** (easiest for non-designers; has direct KDP-size templates, built-in image placement, and can export print-ready PDFs with bleed) |

## Layout Strategy: Three Visual Styles

Mixing these three styles page-to-page creates a high-end rhythm that feels like a premium lifestyle magazine rather than a dense, intimidating textbook.

---

### Style A — "Text Over Photo" (Full Bleed Spread)
> Best for: dramatic chapter openers, "Part" section openers, emotional moments

The most premium look. A single, edge-to-edge photograph spans the entire page or two-page spread. Strategic **negative space** (an intentionally soft, out-of-focus area) on one side is where text is placed directly on-top of the image.

**How to do it:**
- When generating AI images, add the phrases **`"negative space on the left third"`, `"copy space"`, or `"blurred out-of-focus background on the right"`** to your prompts.
- Place text directly on the smooth, blurred area. Use **white text + subtle drop shadow**, or a **semi-transparent dark overlay box** behind the text, to ensure it is readable for senior eyes.

**Example prompt addition:**
```
...photorealistic, cinematic 85mm lens, shallow depth-of-field,
soft out-of-focus warm beige wall on the LEFT THIRD for text copy space,
subject on the right side, no text, 300 DPI, 2100x3000 pixels
```

---

### Style B — "Magazine Split" (White Page + Photo Page)
> Best for: chapters with more text, step-by-step instructions, glossary, takeaways

A clean 50/50 left-right split. Maximum readability.

**How to do it:**
- Left page: Pure white or very warm cream background (hex `#FDFBF7`). Text ONLY.
- Right page: Full-bleed photorealistic image bleeding to all five edges (including bleed area).

---

### Style C — "Section Divider" (Dramatic Headline Over Photo)
> Best for: Part 1, Part 2, Part 3 section title pages

One massive, stunning photograph fills the entire page. A single very large bold headline (60pt+) is centered in the middle, in white text with a subtle shadow. No body text. Pure visual impact.

**Example:**
- Full-page photo of a glowing city skyline at dusk.
- Centered text: **"PART 2: GETTING HANDS-ON WITH AI"** in 72pt white Playfair Display Bold.

## Typography Rules for Seniors

Readability is the absolute highest priority. A beautiful layout fails if the text is too small.

| Element | Rule |
|---|---|
| **Body Text Font** | Clean, modern Sans-Serif: **Inter**, **Roboto**, or **Lato** (free via Google Fonts) |
| **Chapter Title Font** | Strong, elegant Serif: **Merriweather Bold** or **Playfair Display** |
| **Body Text Size** | Minimum **14pt–16pt**. Do NOT go below 14pt for any running text. |
| **Chapter Title Size** | **36pt or larger**. Be bold and confident. |
| **Contrast** | Always extreme contrast. Black text on white/cream. White text on dark photo areas only. Never gray-on-gray. |
| **Line Spacing** | 1.4× line height minimum for readability. |

## Per-Chapter Image Prompt Template

Use the following template as a starting point for each chapter's AI image. Replace the `[SCENE DESCRIPTION]` with the specific visual for that spread.

```
Photorealistic, high-quality magazine-style photograph.
[SCENE DESCRIPTION].
Warm, natural cinematic lighting. 85mm full-frame lens.
[copy space on the LEFT / subject on the RIGHT] — OR — [Full bleed, subject centered].
Realistic faces, detailed real-world environment, professional photography.
No text, no watermarks.
Minimum 300 DPI, 2100 x 3000 pixels.
```

**Example (Chapter 7 — Installing the App):**
```
Photorealistic, high-quality magazine-style photograph.
Close-up of a senior man's gentle hand tapping the "Install" button on a
modern smartphone screen. ChatGPT app store page visible, clean and bright.
Warm, natural lighting, shallow depth-of-field.
Copy space on the LEFT for text overlay.
Realistic skin, high detail. No text, no watermarks.
Minimum 300 DPI, 2100 x 3000 pixels.
```
