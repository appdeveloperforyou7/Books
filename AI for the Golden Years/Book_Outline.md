# AI for the Golden Years
## Your Friendly Guide to Everyday Magic and Staying Safe

## Target Audience
Seniors aged 55+, who use smartphones and the internet but may feel overwhelmed or confused by the sudden rise of "AI" in the news and daily life.

## Format Specifications
- **Trim Size**: 7 x 10 inches (ideal for photo-heavy premium books on Amazon KDP)
- **Interior**: Premium Color Print, 300 DPI images minimum
- **Length**: ~44 pages
- **Layout**: Three alternating styles (see Layout_and_Design_Guide.md for full details):
  - Style A: Full-bleed photo with text on negative/copy space (most dramatic)
  - Style B: White text page (left) paired with full-bleed photo (right)
  - Style C: Section divider: dramatic full-bleed photo + single large headline only
- **Tone**: Respectful, encouraging, clear (no jargon), cautious but optimistic.
