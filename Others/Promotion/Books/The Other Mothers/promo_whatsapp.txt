👁️ *The Other Mothers — A Missing Child. Three Women With Secrets. A Past That Refuses to Stay Buried.*

A boy goes missing on the first day of term. The yard goes quiet. The parents look at each other and see strangers.

For Nora, it is the end of everything she built — and the beginning of a truth she has spent twenty-two years running from. For the women standing at the railings, it is the moment the past finally catches up.

Some secrets are buried for a reason. But the ground remembers.

A gripping, emotionally devastating psychological thriller about the stories mothers tell themselves to survive — and the lies that unravel when a child disappears.
