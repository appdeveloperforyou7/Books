🎭 *The Devoted — The Perfect Murder Is One No One Knows Is a Crime.*

A celebrated philanthropist dies of her own sleeping pills. Suicide, everyone says.

But executive coach Elena Vance never believes the face everyone else is reading. When she meets the dead woman's inner circle, she notices the micro-expression that doesn't fit — the grief that's a little too polished, a little too perfect.

Everyone heard Maren's music playing at midnight. Everyone assumed she was asleep.
Everyone is wrong.

In a room full of women who all loved her, Elena must find the one who ensured Maren would never wake up.

Book 2 in the Elena Vance series. Fair-play psychological detective fiction.
