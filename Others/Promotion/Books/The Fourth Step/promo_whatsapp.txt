🌑 *The Fourth Step — Some Silences Last Twenty-Five Years.*

You are twelve years old. The hallway is cold. The window at the end is open. And you see something you were never meant to see.

Twenty-five years later, Neve returns to the house by the sea.

What happened that night never left her. And now the silence is finally breaking.

A literary psychological thriller that coils from the first page and tightens until you can barely breathe. If you love atmospheric, character-driven suspense with a fracture at its center, this one is for you.
