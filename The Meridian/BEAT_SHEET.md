## PACING & CRAFT CHECKS
- **Hook variety audit:** alternate revelation / question / foreboding / image / cliffhanger — no two adjacent chapters end the same way. (Tagged per chapter above.)
- **Open-loop rule:** every twist payoff opens ≥2 new questions (e.g., T3 closes "what's the prize" → opens "who is ELVIA?" + "do we sabotage?").
- **Clock discipline:** timestamps strictly increase; Act III compresses (real-time) to spike adrenaline.
- **POV balance:** Sloane ~40%, then Jae/Naïma/Theo/Emeka/Vesna rotating; no POV dominates two acts without relief.
- **Clean-content guardrail:** all violence implied (Ch22 implied body, Ch35 sealed-machine menace, Ch38 trapping — no gore).
