G. PRICING & ROYALTY  (US marketplace)
================================================================================
KDP print cost (B&W, cream, 8.5x11 LARGE FORMAT, 109+ pages):
  8.5x11 is a higher per-page tier (~$0.030/page) than 6x9.
  cost = fixed + (pages x ~$0.030)
  172 pages -> ~$6.00      173 pages -> ~$6.03
  (Confirm the exact figure on KDP's Print Cost calculator.)

Royalty (60% rule):  Royalty = (List Price x 0.60) - Print Cost
  Minimum list price (60%) = print cost / 0.60  ->  ~$10.00
  *** KDP enforces a ~$10.00 floor; you CANNOT price below it at 60%. ***

  List price   Your royalty (print ~$6.00)
  ----------   ----------------------------
   $10.00         $0.00   (minimum KDP allows)
   $10.99         $0.59
   $11.99         $1.19
   $12.99         $1.79
   $13.99         $2.39
   $14.99         $2.99
   $15.99         $3.59
   $16.99         $4.19
   $17.99         $4.79
   $19.99         $5.99

Recommendation:
  - List price:    $14.99  (royalty ~$2.99) - good margin, still competitive
  - Launch/promo:  $12.99  (royalty ~$1.79) - lowest sensible, above the $10 floor
  - Premium feel:  $16.99  (royalty ~$4.19)
  - Keep the SAME price on all 10 volumes for a clean series.

Expanded Distribution (40% rule):  Royalty = (List Price x 0.40) - Print Cost
  - Requires your OWN ISBN (free KDP ISBN blocks it).
  - Higher floor: min list = print / 0.40  ->  ~$15.00
  - To actually earn on ED, price >= $16.99 (royalty ~$0.80 at $16.99).

Other marketplaces:
  - KDP auto-suggests UK / DE / FR / etc. from the US price; review their
    minimums (they differ and can be higher per marketplace).
