# Senior Large-Print Activity Book Series — Build Plan

**Companion documents:** `RESEARCH-Senior-Activity-Book-Market.md` (market evidence) · `CONTENTS-Senior-Activity-Book-Series.md` (canonical activity counts, difficulty tiers, grid specs, page budget) · `LAYOUT-and-Tooling-Senior-Activity-Book-Series.md` (typography/layout spec + PDF-tool decision) · `ratings.md` (competitive scoring rubric vs top 3).

## Goal
Produce a 10-book series of premium large-print activity books for seniors, sold via KDP (Amazon) to a **global English audience (US primary)**. Single criterion: **maximize unit volume**. Strategy: **premium content, undercut on price** (value-leader), differentiated via nostalgia theming + a unique "Home Remedies & Wellness Wisdom" page per book.

## Market research findings (evidence)
- "Large print activity books for seniors" → 9,000+ listings; "large print word search for seniors" → 6,000+. **High demand, high competition** → differentiation + keyword targeting + reviews required; do NOT fight generic incumbents head-on (e.g. Martin Littlewolf dominates generic word search).
- Bestseller patterns: 120–224 pages; trim 8.5×11 or 8×10; **word search dominates**, **variety books** (word search + sudoku + mazes + scramble + trivia) sell strongly as "brain exercise" hooks; title keywords that convert = "Large Print / Extra Large / Jumbo / XXL", puzzle count, themed, "for Seniors", "perfect gift"; solutions grouped at back; high-contrast black on white.
- Nostalgia (1950s/60s/70s) is a proven differentiator; "Volume"/year-edition series create repeat buyers.
- Two sub-markets: "active seniors/brain health" (bigger, competitive) vs "dementia/Alzheimer's cognitive care" (simpler, photo/reminiscence, lower page count). **This series targets the "active seniors / brain health" market** (largest volume).

## Series concept & positioning
- **Imprint/brand:** *Bright Mind Press* (adjustable).
- **Series:** *Bright Mind Large-Print Puzzles & Brain Games* — branded themed series. The **category leads** ("MEMORY & BRAIN GAMES FOR SENIORS") on every cover; the theme is secondary and always paired with the volume number.
- Differentiation = **quality, variety, accessibility, value, and a unique Home Remedies page** (not nostalgia). Every book shares an identical premium spec; only theme + accent color + themed content differ. One production setup reused 10×.
- **All content evergreen / globally relatable** (no decade/era/country-specific references).

## Per-book spec (identical for all 10)
- **Trim:** 8.5×11 in, cream paper, B&W interior, matte cover.
- **Pages:** ~185 (front matter 6 + activity pages ~141 incl. 2 Home Remedies pages + solutions ~36 + back matter 2). Final count verified after layout. (See page-budget reconciliation in `CONTENTS-Senior-Activity-Book-Series.md`.)
- **Large print & typography (full spec in `LAYOUT-` doc):** body **18 pt** (**Atkinson Hyperlegible Next**, high-x-height low-vision font, SIL OFL; floor 16 pt never below); grid letters 20–22 pt, sudoku numbers 24–26 pt; leading 1.5; true black on cream; jumbo grids; all fonts embedded (use static TTFs only).
- **Layout:** KDP-correct bleed, gutter, safe margins (use KDP 8.5×11 template). One puzzle per page for word search / crosswords / mazes; 2 per page for sudoku / scramble / trivia.
- **Puzzle mix per book — exactly 180 puzzles (cover seal "180+ Brain Games" is literally true):**
  - Word Search (themed, large print): 54
  - Sudoku (easy–challenger, large print, unique solution): 36
  - Word Scramble (themed): 30
  - Trivia + "Finish the Phrase": 24
  - Crosswords (themed, interlocking, solver-verified): 18
  - Mazes (large, easy): 18
  - Bonus coloring pages (simple line art): 4 pages (extra, not counted in the 180)
  - Full solutions grouped at back.
- **Difficulty framework (3 tiers, all senior-appropriate; no "Hard/Expert"):** Easy (warm-up) / Medium (steady) / Challenger (gentle stretch). Per-type counts by tier, grid sizes, and hints are defined in `CONTENTS-Senior-Activity-Book-Series.md`. Distribution across the book: Easy 79 · Medium 63 · Challenger 38 (Easy-skewed ≈79% E+M so the book feels welcoming).

## "Wow" feature — Home Remedies & Wellness Wisdom (2 pages)
- **Two pages per book**, each entry = a **condition + 2–3 line explanation** (e.g., Minor Indigestion, Mild Headache, Sore Throat, Nasal Congestion, Trouble Sleeping, Mild Muscle Ache, Dry Cough). Universal, non-prescriptive folk/home care.
- **HARD CONSTRAINT: the word "Ayurveda" must NOT appear anywhere** — not on cover, interior, or metadata. Present as universal traditional/folk "home remedies".
- **Mandatory disclaimer on every remedies page:** "Traditional home remedies shared for cultural enjoyment and general wellbeing. This is not medical advice; consult a healthcare professional." Framed as folk wisdom, never prescriptive, never dosing-based.

## Pricing strategy (KDP paperback, US; 60% royalty − print)
- **Trim = 8.5×11 = KDP "Large Trim"** (>6.12″ wide or >9″ tall) → B&W rate is **$1.00 fixed + $0.017/page**.
- Print cost @ **172 pp** ≈ **$3.92** ($1.00 + 172×$0.017). Minimum list price (floor) ≈ **$6.54**.
- **Permanent list: $9.99** (royalty ~$2.07) — competitive with the premium-variety band, now viable after compressing solutions (210→172 pp).
- **Launch promo: $8.99 for first 60–90 days** (royalty ~$1.47) to seed BSR + reviews, then raise to $9.99 (or $10.99 for ~$2.67 margin).
- **Do not** price near the floor: $7.99 ≈ $0.87/unit, $6.99 ≈ $0.27. Ultra-low prices also cheapen the premium/gift positioning.
- **India caveat:** KDP does **not** distribute paperbacks on Amazon.in (no INR marketplace). Low ₹ books there are offset-printed, a different model.
- Page-reduction levers already applied: dropped word-search answer keys (finding = the puzzle), crossword/sudoku/maze solutions at 4/page, coloring 4→2, contents page removed.

## Cover system (family template — category leads)
- One master template; only **accent color + theme + hero illustration** vary between the 10 books. Everything else is fixed so the series reads as one family.
- Front cover hierarchy (top→bottom): **LARGE PRINT** banner → **"MEMORY & BRAIN GAMES FOR SENIORS"** (dominant title) → **"Volume N – Theme"** (downgraded, paired) → warm **AI hero of a consistent elderly couple** doing puzzles (FLUX.1-schnell via HF, fixed seed) → crisp **puzzle-type tiles** (word search/sudoku/crossword/maze) → **gold "180+ Brain Games" seal** → imprint.
- Spine leads with the category ("MEMORY & BRAIN GAMES / VOL N – THEME / imprint") so the memory identity isn't lost behind the theme. Back cover: blurb + "Volume N – Theme" + clear barcode zone (KDP prints its own).
- **Thumbnail-legibility rule:** "LARGE PRINT" + "MEMORY & BRAIN GAMES" must read clearly at mobile thumbnail size (high contrast, large type).
- Export **full cover** (front + spine + back + bleed) using KDP cover calculator against the final page count (spine width ≈ pages × 0.0025″ cream; spine text only at ≥80 pp). AI imagery → disclose at KDP publish.

## Quality bar (non-negotiable)
- **Every puzzle auto-verified solvable** (solver-checkers for word search, sudoku, word scramble); sudoku must have a unique solution.
- No duplicate words within a book; themed word banks curated (avoid generic AI sameness).
- Font-size, margin, bleed, and gutter checks before export.
- Solutions complete and correct.

## Production approach (for the build session — generate all 10 in one go)
**Engine: Python + ReportLab** (chosen over Typst/WeasyPrint/LaTeX/FPDF2/Puppeteer/InDesign for full Canvas-level geometry control, vector puzzle grids, font embedding, determinism, and batch scale — see the tool evaluation in `LAYOUT-` doc). Generator modules:
1. **Project setup:** deps, config (the spec table in `LAYOUT-`: trim 8.5×11, bleed 0.125″, margins/gutter, **Atkinson Hyperlegible Next** font — static TTFs from `Atkinson_Hyperlegible_Next/static/`, KDP templates), theme registry.
2. **Themed content banks:** curated word lists, trivia, maze seeds, remedy sets per theme (10 themes) — volumes sized to the per-type counts in `CONTENTS-` (WS 54 / Sdk 45 / Scramble 36 / Trivia+Phrase 27 / Mazes 18).
3. **Puzzle generators + solvers (verified):** word search, sudoku (unique solution), word scramble, trivia/finish-the-phrase, mazes, coloring line art — each generator emits the Easy/Medium/Challenger split defined in `CONTENTS-`.
4. **Remedies module:** per-theme safe remedy sets + disclaimer renderer (no "Ayurveda").
5. **Front/back matter:** title, copyright, welcome/how-to-use, TOC, series page, notes.
6. **Layout engine:** 8.5×11, large print, solutions at back, page-budget assembler to hit ~175pp (see page-budget table in `CONTENTS-`).
7. **Cover renderer:** template + per-theme accents + badges → full KDP cover PDF.
8. **Metadata exporter:** per-book title/subtitle/keywords/categories → CSV.
9. **Batch runner:** loop themes → 10 interiors + 10 covers + 10 metadata files.
10. **QA pass:** solver-verify every puzzle; render + margin + font-size checks; visually sample each PDF.

## Risks & mitigations
- **Generic-incumbent collision:** mitigated by themes + price + remedies wow-factor.
- **1-star from a broken/wrong puzzle:** mitigated by mandatory solver verification + solution audit.
- **Medical-advice liability / Amazon policy:** mitigated by folk-wisdom framing, no "Ayurveda" wording, disclaimer on every remedies page, non-prescriptive content.
- **AI sameness / thin differentiation:** mitigated by curated themed banks, varied layouts, accent system.
- **Low-content scrutiny:** variety books with real puzzles are not low-content; ensure ≥109 pages and genuine activity variety.

## Validation (before publishing each book)
- All solver checks pass (0 unsolvable puzzles); solution keys 100% correct.
- PDF page count ~175; trim 8.5×11; margins/bleed/gutter conform to KDP template.
- Body/grid font ≥ 16pt; high contrast.
- Cover thumbnails legible; badges present; spine width matches page count.
- Metadata CSV complete; no "Ayurveda" anywhere (grep the full output).
