# Competitive Ratings — Senior Large-Print Puzzle Book Segment

**Purpose:** define every parameter that makes a great senior large-print puzzle book; score the **top 3 competitor archetypes** against **our *Bright Mind Large-Print Puzzles & Brain Games*** series; and surface where we win, where we trail, and how to close the gaps.

**Evidence base:** in-repo market research (`RESEARCH-Senior-Activity-Book-Market.md`) + our built/verified spec (`PLAN-`, `CONTENTS-`, `LAYOUT-` docs). Competitor entries are **representative bestseller archetypes** (real titles vary); our entry is our actual, independently-QA'd per-book spec.

**Companion files:** `PLAN-…`, `RESEARCH-…`, `CONTENTS-…`, `LAYOUT-and-Tooling-…`.

---

## 1. Methodology
- **Scale:** each sub-parameter scored **0–5** (0 absent · 1 poor · 2 below avg · 3 meets bar · 4 strong · 5 best-in-class).
- **7 weighted categories** sum to **100**; category points = (avg of sub-scores ÷ 5) × weight.
- Weights reflect what matters most **for seniors**: legibility first, then quality/accuracy (1-star risk), then variety/brain-exercise value.
- N/A sub-parameters (e.g., sudoku for a word-search-only book) are excluded from that book's category average.
- **Caveat:** our score reflects **designed spec + independent solver QA (0 errors)**, not market traction. At launch our review/brand signals are weak (Category F) — the score assumes the spec is executed as built.

## 6. Price-per-puzzle value check (large-trim reality)

| Book | Puzzles | Price | $ / puzzle |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| C1 Giant WS incumbent | 224 | ~$8.99 | **$0.040** (best) |
| Ours (launch) | 180 | $8.99 | $0.050 |
| Ours (list) | 180 | $9.99 | $0.056 |
| C2 Premium variety | ~154 | ~$10.99 | $0.071 |
| C3 Themed | ~80 | ~$8.99 | $0.112 |

**Takeaway:** 8.5×11 is KDP **large trim** (~$3.92 print at 172 pp after compressing solutions), so we can't undercut the single-type giant on raw $/puzzle — and shouldn't try. We **beat the premium-variety and themed books**, and the $9.99 list (down from a planned $10.99) is now competitive while keeping a healthy ~$2.07 royalty. Win on **value-per-dollar of quality/variety** (6 types, verified, remedies), not cheapest count.

## 7. Our honest gaps & gap-closing actions
| Gap | Why it hurts | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **F4 — zero reviews at launch** | Biggest real disadvantage; buyers trust social proof. | ARC/early-copy seeding, KDP launch coupons, Amazon Ads to seed first 25–50 verified reviews in 60–90 days. |
| **F1 — $/puzzle trails the giant** | Large-trim cost forces $10.99 list. | Win on variety+quality+remedies, not puzzle count. Optional: compress solutions (4/page) to trim ~18pp → lower cost → could price $9.99. |
| **C1 giant's brand/recognition** | New imprint vs Best Seller. | Build the Bright Mind series identity (couple mascots, gold seal, 10-volume collector set, category-led covers). |
| **India retail** | KDP has no paperback distribution on Amazon.in. | Separate offset-print-in-India track if India retail is a goal (out of scope for KDP POD). |

## 8. Where we win outright (defend in copy & cover)
- **Legibility (A):** Atkinson Hyperlegible Next, ≥18pt, jumbo grids, cream, KDP gutter — best-in-class.
- **Variety + difficulty (B):** 6 activity types (incl. crosswords many rivals lack), 3-tier — strongest brain-exercise mix.
- **Brand (C):** category-led covers, consistent couple mascots, gold seal, 10-theme family.
- **Quality (D):** solver-verified **and independently QA'd — 0 errors across 1,800 puzzles**; unique-solution sudoku; zero word repetition.
- **Giftability (G):** warm elderly-couple mascots + "Perfect Gift for a Sharp Mind" + 10-volume collector series.
- **Production (E):** 210pp heft, KDP-verified geometry/embedded fonts, premium covers.
